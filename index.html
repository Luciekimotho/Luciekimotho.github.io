I had every intention of posting this on the first day of 2016 but life got in the way and I just let it. I’m going to try to be as honest as possible today (and every day really since it’s one of my goals) and get this year started on an honest note. Making: plans to rule the world. Sorta! I usually have one year goals set out but for the first time in a long time, I’m working on a five year strategy. Granted it will be as flexible as possible but I believe having a rough idea of what I want for my life will give me bigger goals to work towards on on a personal and professional level. Eating: more home cooked meals now that I’ve got my own home! I think I’ve shared before that the kitchen has never been my favourite place but the idea of learning new meals in this new space really excites me so I’ve been whipping out Food Network recipes and calling over friends who happen to be great cooks to teach me new tricks. Drinking: more and more green tea. Reading: Desert Flower by Waris Dirie. This book makes me question whether all my life’s frustrations are really as big as they seem. Wanting: more honest, open conversations with the people I love most in this world. Playing: board games with friends on a casual Friday night should be a monthly activity as far as I’m concerned.
